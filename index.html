Robert Jordan & Brandon Sanderson · WOT

The Wheel of Time turns, and Ages come and pass, leaving memories that become legend. The Ideas

the four wheels the whole saga turns on

The Wheel & the Pattern

time as a wheel of seven spokes

  • The Wheel of Time turns, and Ages come and pass — what was, will be again; there is neither beginning nor end to the turning.
  • It weaves the Pattern of the Ages from the threads of human lives — and a thread cannot see the whole of the weave.

The One Power

saidin and saidar

  • Drawn from the True Source, the Power is split male and female, woven in five threads — Earth, Air, Fire, Water, Spirit.
  • The Dark One's counterstroke tainted saidin at the Breaking; for three thousand years, any man who channeled went mad.

The Dragon Reborn

the champion reborn, and the Last Battle

  • The soul of the Dragon is spun out again whenever the Wheel needs him — to face the Dark One at Tarmon Gai'don.
  • The Prophecies of the Dragon foretell that he will break the world to save it, and bleed on the rocks of Shayol Ghul.

ta'veren, the Ajahs & the Aiel

the Pattern-benders and the peoples

  • Ta'veren are the points the Pattern bends around — and three walked the world at once in this Age.
  • The White Tower's seven Ajahs and the spear-bearing Aiel of the Three-fold Land are the saga's two great orders.

The Main Sequence

the fourteen volumes of the turning Wheel

  1. 1 · The Eye of the World1990three young men flee the Two Rivers as the Shadow hunts them — the journey to the Eye
  2. 2 · The Great Hunt1990the Horn of Valere stolen; the hunt across the Portal Stones; Rand proclaimed the Dragon
  3. 3 · The Dragon Reborn1991Rand seizes the crystal sword Callandor in the Stone of Tear, fulfilling prophecy
  4. 4 · The Shadow Rising1992Rhuidean and the hidden history of the Aiel; the Two Rivers stands against the Shadow
  5. 5 · The Fires of Heaven1993across the Waste; Rand against Couladin; Moiraine into the doorway
  6. 6 · Lord of Chaos1994the cage at Dumai's Wells — “Let the Lord of Chaos rule.”
  7. 7 · A Crown of Swords1996the Bowl of the Winds, and the cleansing of the taint draws near
  8. 8 · The Path of Daggers1998the Bowl breaks the unnatural winter as the Seanchan press inward
  9. 9 · Winter's Heart2000Rand cleanses saidin of the Dark One's taint at Shadar Logoth
  10. 10 · Crossroads of Twilight2003the world holds its breath in the wake of the cleansing
  11. 11 · Knife of Dreams2005the last volume Jordan wrote; the pace quickens; Mat weds Tuon
  12. 12 · The Gathering Storm2009Sanderson takes up the notes; Rand at his darkest on Dragonmount; Egwene reforges the Tower
  13. 13 · Towers of Midnight2010Mat into the Tower of Ghenjei; Perrin's reckoning; the threads draw together
  14. 14 · A Memory of Light2013Tarmon Gai'don — the Last Battle at Shayol Ghul
